Everything about our Metal Cards

Clear answers about our premium metal NFC cards.

Are the metal cards heavy?

Yes, and that's intentional! The substantial weight (approximately 25g) creates a premium feel that immediately communicates quality. It's noticeably heavier than plastic but comfortably weighted for daily use.

How are they manufactured?

Each card is CNC-machined from solid metal blocks for precision and durability. We use aerospace-grade metals that are then polished, finished, and laser-engraved to create a flawless, permanent result.

Can metal cards be engraved?

Absolutely! We offer silver and gold engraving options. The engraving is laser-etched into the metal surface, creating permanent, crisp details that won't fade or wear off over time.

Do they interfere with NFC?

No. We use a specialized layered construction that allows the NFC chip to function perfectly through the metal. The cards work just as reliably as plastic cards with all modern smartphones.

How long does production take?

Metal cards take 7-10 working days due to the precision manufacturing process. Each card is individually machined, polished, engraved, and quality-checked to ensure it meets our luxury standards.
