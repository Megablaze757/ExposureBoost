Everything about our Wooden Cards

Clear answers about our sustainable wooden NFC cards.

Are the wooden cards durable?

Yes! Our wooden cards are treated with a protective food-grade oil finish that makes them water-resistant and durable for everyday use. They're designed to last for years while maintaining their beautiful appearance.

Is the wood sustainably sourced?

Absolutely. All our wood comes from sustainably managed forests and we use offcuts from larger furniture projects, minimizing waste. Each card tells a story of responsible sourcing.

How does NFC work in wood?

We embed a thin NFC chip between layers of wood, then seal it with our protective finish. The wood doesn't interfere with the NFC signal, so it works just as reliably as plastic or metal cards.

Can I get a custom wood type?

Yes! We offer 8 premium wood types (Bamboo, Oak, Maple, Wood Base, Black Bamboo, Walnut, Cherry, Sapele) and can accommodate custom requests for larger orders. Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss exotic woods, custom thickness, or special grain requirements for your brand.

How long does production take?

Wood cards take 5-7 working days as each is hand-finished to highlight the natural grain. The extra time ensures each card is a unique work of art that meets our quality standards.
