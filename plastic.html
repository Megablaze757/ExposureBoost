Absolutely! Our plastic cards use high-quality PVC or recycled plastic that's scratch-resistant, waterproof, and designed to withstand daily use. They're as durable as credit cards and will last for years.

Can I print full-color designs on plastic cards?

Yes! Plastic cards offer the best printing capabilities. We use high-resolution digital printing that supports full-color designs, photos, gradients, and intricate details with excellent color reproduction.

What's the minimum order quantity?

We accept orders from a single card up to thousands. Single cards are perfect for testing or personal use, while bulk orders (50+) receive significant discounts. Contact us for custom bulk pricing.

How long does printing and delivery take?

Standard production time is 3-5 working days after design approval. Delivery times depend on location but are typically 2-3 days within the UK. Rush production (24-48 hours) is available for urgent orders.
